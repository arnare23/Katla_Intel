Our Story

From Research to Real-World Impact

Katla Intel was founded by two Masters students in Financial Engineering who saw firsthand how deep learning could transform industries beyond finance. During their studies specializing in neural networks and quantitative modeling, they recognized that many corporations struggled to adopt AI -- not for lack of need, but for lack of accessible expertise.

Named after one of Iceland's most powerful volcanoes, Katla Intel embodies the transformative energy beneath the surface -- the potential within every organization's data waiting to be unlocked.

Today, we work with corporations and smaller projects alike, bringing the same rigorous, research-driven approach to every engagement.
